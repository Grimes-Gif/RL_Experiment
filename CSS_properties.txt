CSS properties that aren't self explanatory

Overflow:
The overflow property specifies whether to clip content,
render scroll bars or display overflow content when
content overflows the element's content area.
values: 	auto | hidden | scroll | visible | initial | inherit

Margin:
property that sets the margin on all four sides of the element
values: 	[ length | percentage | auto ] 1 to 4 values | initial | inherit
